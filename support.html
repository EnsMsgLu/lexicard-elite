Frequently Asked Questions

üì± How do I add new words?

Tap the + button on the home screen or library tab. Enter the word and definition, then save.

üîÑ How does spaced repetition work?

Words you know well appear less frequently. Words you struggle with appear more often. This optimizes your learning!

üìä What is the daily session?

Each day, you get 25 words to study: 5 review words (streak 3+) and 20 new/learning words. Complete all to build your streak!

üîä Why isn't text-to-speech working?

Make sure your device volume is up and TTS is enabled in your device settings. You can adjust speech speed in Profile > Voice Speed.

üíæ Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. Use the Export feature in Profile to backup your words to a file.

üîî How do reminders work?

Enable Daily Reminders in Profile. You'll get a notification 12 hours after your last app usage to remind you to study.

üóëÔ∏è How do I delete my data?

Go to Profile > Clear All Data. This will remove all words, progress, and settings. This action cannot be undone.
